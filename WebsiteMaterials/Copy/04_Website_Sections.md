## About The Game

Knucklebones is a dark fantasy strategy game about rolling, assigning, and enduring. Each die can solve one problem while leaving another exposed. Combat, encounters, and route choices all demand tradeoffs, and the board itself can lift you toward safety or cast you back into danger.

## Why It Stands Out

- The board is not a menu. Traversal itself creates tension.
- Dice are a build system, not just a randomizer.
- The game's visual identity is tactile and authored rather than generic fantasy UI.
- Runs are short enough to replay, but sharp enough to leave a mark.

## Encounter Flavor

Strange figures and symbolic spaces shape the run: shrines, traps, ashen camps, gatehouses, ramblers, dwellers, and hostile creatures that test both planning and nerve.
