Knucklebones is a dark tactical dice builder that sends players across a snakes-and-ladders roguelike board where every roll must be assigned, every path is a gamble, and every run leaves a scar.

Knucklebones combines board traversal, tactical dice placement, and roguelike buildcraft into short, punishing runs. Players roll, assign, upgrade, and sacrifice their way across a hostile path of combat, thresholds, bargains, and sudden reversals.
