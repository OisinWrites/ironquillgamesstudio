# Core Features

- Tactical dice assignment where every roll must be committed deliberately.
- A snakes-and-ladders inspired roguelike map with branching routes, rises, and setbacks.
- Threshold-based combat and encounter resolution that rewards planning over panic.
- Dice-building progression with upgrades, altered faces, replacements, and tradeoffs.
- Distinct event encounters, including bespoke folkloric figures such as the Dweller and the Rambler.
- Short repeatable runs built around pressure, scarcity, and meaningful decisions.
- A tactile dark-fantasy presentation combining parchment, paper, carved-board motifs, and illustrated event art.

## Design Pillars

- Scarcity over excess
- Consequence over spectacle
- Tension through placement
- Folkloric atmosphere with tactile board-game presentation

## Audience Positioning

Knucklebones should appeal to players who enjoy:

- tactical roguelikes
- dice strategy
- board-game inspired digital games
- risk-reward pathing
- compact but punishing runs
